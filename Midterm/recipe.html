Perfect Fried Egg

by Epicurious

Everything you need

2 Eggs
10-20g Butter
Salt & Pepper
1

Heating the Pan

Place your non-stick pan on the stove over medium-high heat.

2

Adding Butter

Add a bit of butter to the pan to grease it initially. This prevents the eggs from sticking and adds flavor.

3

Cracking the Eggs

Crack the eggs on a flat surface rather than the edge of the pan or a bowl. This technique helps avoid getting shell fragments in the eggs.

4

Seasoning

Season the eggs with salt and pepper to taste while they cook.

5

More butter

Add a significant amount of butter to baste the eggs, focusing on the whites to set them while avoiding the yolk.

6

Basting

Baste with butter to achieve crispy edges and a well-cooked white, keeping the yolk runny.

7

Drain

Once the edges are crispy and the white is set, remove from heat. Use a spatula to carefully place the eggs on a plate, accompanied by toast to dip into the yolk.

8

Enjoy!

Enjoy your perfectly cooked fried eggs as Chef Proto suggests, savoring the combination of crispy edges and soft, runny yolk with the simplicity of the buttery flavor.
