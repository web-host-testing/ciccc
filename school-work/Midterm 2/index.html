How people eat

Fried Eggs

America

Classic American breakfast plate

This breakfast typically includes a fried egg or two served alongside bacon or sausage, toast, and hash browns or home fries. It's a staple in diners and homes across the country.

Spain

Huevos con Chorizo

A simple yet popular breakfast dish where eggs are fried and served alongside chorizo. The eggs might be fried in the oil rendered from cooking the chorizo, infusing them with flavor.

Japan

Fried egg with rice

Fried eggs are placed over hot steamed rice. The yolk, when broken, acts as a rich, creamy sauce that blends with the rice.
